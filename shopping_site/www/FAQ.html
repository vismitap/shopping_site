FAQ

1. What is the specialty of wearing Babu Fashion cotton Tshirt?

Cotton is a ‘natural’ fibre, which means it is not derived using chemicals.
For someone with sensitive skin, or for children, polyester might be an irritant and cause chafing of the skin.
Mercerised cotton on the other hand is softer and more absorbent, resulting in lesser to no irritation of the skin.
This is especially important when choosing your garments.
Our 100% cotton Shirts, T-Shirts & socks are very light and soft on your skin, and won’t make your body sweat in normal conditions unlike the polyester garments.
While the fit is important, choosing the right material goes a long way in ensuring extra comfort.
Mercerisation also makes the product lustrous and soft adding not only comfort but luxury to your feet.
Mercerised cotton products also stand the tests of time and several washes, and will make your Apparels fit and look almost as good as new over a long period of time.

25. What does SSL mean, how do I know the website is safe?

Babu Fashion uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.
This means your data is encrypted and protected when you send data to Babu Fashion over the Internet.
You can tell a site uses and has enabled SSL when you see a padlock at the top of your browser.
You will also notice that the location field at the top of the browser will be with ‘https’ instead of the normal ‘http’.
This means you are in secure mode.
